About Roll-24

Roll-24 is a slow-photography app that mimics the feel of a 24-exposure film camera. You get 24 shots per roll, choose a filter before each shot, and your photos stay hidden until they develop at midnight, just like dropping film off at a lab. Pin your favorites to the corkboard, unlock premium filters in the Shop, or subscribe to Directors for unlimited rolls.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I develop my photos?

Roll-24 photos automatically develop at midnight (00:00) local time. Open the Develop screen after midnight to see your finished roll. In a hurry? Tap the develop button 5 times to start an instant countdown and develop the roll early.

2. Why am I limited to 24 shots?

That's the point. Roll-24 recreates the discipline of analog film: 24 frames per roll forces you to slow down, compose, and make each shot count. Need more? You can buy 32 extra shots in the Shop for $0.99, or subscribe to Directors for unlimited rolls.

3. What's included in Premium?

The free version includes the Standard and Black & White filters. Premium unlocks the Gold200 and Tokyo filters (one-time purchase, $3.99). Other Shop items include 32 extra shots ($0.99), the Archive expansion ($1.99), and the Directors monthly subscription ($4.99/month) which gives unlimited rolls.

4. How do I restore my purchases?

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, tap Restore Purchases in either the Shop or Settings screen. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

5. How do I cancel my Directors subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not by Roll-24. On your iPhone, open Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions, tap Roll-24 Directors, then tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

6. How do I delete my account?

Open Roll-24, go to Settings, and find the Delete Account button at the bottom. Press and hold for 1.5 seconds to confirm. This permanently deletes your account, photos, and purchase history from our servers. Active subscriptions must be cancelled separately in iOS Settings.

7. I'm not receiving notifications when my roll develops.

Check that notifications are enabled: iOS Settings → Notifications → Roll-24. Make sure Allow Notifications is on, and that Banners, Sounds, and Badges are enabled if you want all three.

8. The camera won't open / I denied camera access.

Roll-24 needs camera permission to take photos. Open iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera → Roll-24 and toggle it on. Then relaunch the app.

9. Sign In with Apple isn't working.

Roll-24 uses Sign In with Apple as the only sign-in method. If you can't sign in, first check your internet connection. If it still fails, sign out of iCloud (iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Sign Out) and sign back in, then try again. Make sure two-factor authentication is enabled on your Apple ID.

10. My photos aren't uploading or syncing.

Photos sync over the internet. Check that you have a working Wi-Fi or cellular connection, and that iOS Settings → Cellular → Roll-24 is enabled if you're on mobile data. Force-quit the app and reopen it to retry the upload.

11. I forgot which Apple ID I used for Roll-24.

Open the app and go to Settings — your signed-in Apple ID email is shown at the top of the screen. If you're locked out, contact us and we'll help you recover access.

12. I have a question that's not listed here.
